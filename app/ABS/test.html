Common formats

National Record Types


ABSCH-NFP and ABSCH-PA Designation of ABS national focal point and ABS Clearing-House publishing authority download file (doc)

This common format has been prepared for both the designation of the National Focal Point (NFP) of the Protocol and the identification the person responsible for publishing all national records in the Access and Benefit-sharing Clearing-House. The duties of the ABS NFP could be expanded to incorporate the role of publishing national records in the Access and Benefit-sharing Clearing-House.

This common format will only be available offline and their registration can only be done by the Secretariat upon receipt of written communication from:

  • The NFP of the CBD or direct expression of the government for the ABS NFP
  • And if the clearing authority is different from the ABS NFP, its identification has to be endorsed by the NFP.


ABSCH-CNA Competent national authority download file (doc)

This common format has been prepared for facilitating the submission of information on competent national authorities by Parties in order to comply with Article 14 (2) paragraph (b). Where a Party designates more than one competent national authority, the information submitted through this formant needs to include the respective responsibilities of those authorities and specify which competent authority is responsible for the genetic resource sought.


ABSCH-MEA Law, regulations, guidelines, administrative and policy measures download file (doc)

This common format has been prepared for facilitating the submission of information on ABS legislative administrative and policy measures by Parties in order to comply with Article 14 (2) paragraph (a) of the Protocol. The format includes a list of ABS elements and the possibility of pointing out to the specific articles and section where the specific element is addressed.


ABSCH-IRCC Permit or its equivalent constituting and internationally recognized certificate of compliance download file (doc)

This common format is to be used for registering permits or their equivalent issued at the time of access as evidence of the decision to grant prior informed consent and of the establishment of mutually agreed terms. This information is to be made available to the ABS Clearing-House in accordance with paragraph 2 (c) of Article 14.

A permit or its equivalent made available to the ABS Clearing-House shall constitute an internationally recognized certificate of compliance.


ABSCH-CP Checkpoint download file (doc)

This common format is to be used for registering information on checkpoints that collect or receive relevant information related to prior informed consent, to the source of the genetic resource, to the establishment of mutually agreed terms, and/or to the utilization of genetic resources.


ABSCH-CPC Checkpoint Communiqué download file (doc)

This common format is to be used to facilitate the provision of relevant information collected by the checkpoint related to prior informed consent, to the source of the genetic resource, to the establishment of mutually agreed terms, and/or to the utilization of genetic resources, including from the internationally recognized certificate of compliance, when such a certificate is available. Such information needs to be provided to relevant national authorities, to the Party providing prior informed consent and to the ABS Clearing-House, as appropriate.
