ABS-CH MSR Law, regulations, guidelines, administrative and policy measures

This common format has been prepared for facilitating the submission of information on ABS legislative administrative and policy measures by Parties in order to comply with Article 14 (2) paragraph (a) of the Protocol. The format includes a list of ABS elements and the possibility of pointing out to the specific articles and section where the specific element is addressed.
