What is the ABS-CH?

The ABS-CH is a platform for exchanging information on ABS and a key tool for facilitating the implementation of the Nagoya Protocolby enhancing transparency on procedures for access and on the utilization of genetic resources, and in this way increasing the opportunities for benefit-sharing.

Testing the pilot phase

The ABS-CH is currently in its pilot phase and needs your participation and feedback so that it can be fully operational by the time of entry into force of the Nagoya Protocol.

Participate.

The value of the ABS-CH lays in the information published by Parties and others. For the ABS-CH to become an important tool for implementing the Protocol and enhancing transparency, certainty and clarity on access and benefit-sharing, information published by Parties and others needs to be comprehensive and reliable.
