Your ABSCH Roles

You are a Focal Points (ABS-FP) Focal Points (ABS-FP) CBD Primary Focal Points or Nagoya Focal Points have the responsiblity to designate themselves or another user as the Publishing Authority (PA). A Focal Point can complete this offline form and email it to absch@cbd.int to designate or change a PA. For more information, please see Article 13 of the Nagoya Protocol.
YOU ARE THE PUBLISHING AUTHORITY (PA) Publishing Authority (PA) Each country can designate only one PA. The PA's job is to ensure all records are validated before they are published and become publically available on the ABS-CH. PAs can also designate National Authorized Users (NAUs) who are allowed to submit draft records for their country. To designate or change NAUs, the PA can send an email to absch@cbd.int indicating the change, name and email of the NAUs.
You are a National Authorized User (NAU) National Authorized Users (NAU) NAUs can save draft national records and request national records to be published by the Publishing Authority (PA).
YOU ARE A REGISTERED USER REGISTERED USERS Registered Users can save draft reference records and request reference records to be published in the Virtual Library. Reference records are validated by the SCBD before they are published in the ABSCH.
