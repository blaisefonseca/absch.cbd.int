Help with the country profiles

We aim provide all CBD Parties with the help they need to ensure they are able to fulfil their obligations under the Protocol and submit the relevant national information to the ABS clearing-house.

If you are having any difficulties using the ABS clearing-house please contact us and we will be happy to arrange a personal one-on-one training session or any other help as needed.

Email us at: absch@cbd.int
