About CBD accounts

A CBD account can be setup through accounts.cbd.int and gives you access to manage information registered under the different CBD Clearing-Houses including the BCH, ABS-CH and CHM.

Click on any SIGN IN link on a CBD Clearing-House page or click here to sign in now: accounts.cbd.int/signin

Signing in to the ABSCH

Sometimes problems for signing in arise due to compatibility with older versions of internet browsers. At the moment, the ABS-CH works best with the most recent versions of different internet browsers (Firefox, Chrome or Internet Explorer). We will continue working to improve browser compatibility, but in the meanwhile, please make sure you have the most recent version of your favorite browser installed on your computer:

  • For Chrome: https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/
  • For Internet explorer: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-ca/internet-explorer/download-ie
  • For Firefox: http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/

If your problem persists, please contact us at mailto:absch@cbd.int

Forgot your password

You can click here. You will be asked for your email address and, if you are a registered user, an email will be sent immediately to your registered email account with instructions to reset your password.

Updating your profile

Do I need a CBD account for searching information in the ABS-CH

No. Searching information and consulting the country profiles do not require a CBD account.

How do I register for an account

Any person can have a CBD account which will give you access to register information in all CBD clearing-houses including the Biosafety Clearing-House (BCH), the ABS Clearing-House (ABS-CH) and the clearing-house mechanism (CHM) under the Convention. You can register for a CBD account at accounts.cbd.int/signin
