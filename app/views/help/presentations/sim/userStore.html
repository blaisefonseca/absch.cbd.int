You decide to go to Slug and Jiggers Apothecary

YOU are the User

It is your lucky day! The shopkeeper has just a few Sopophorous beans left! He also tells you that these beans are very rare and native to Middle-earth. Elves indigenous to Middle-earth have used these magic beans for centuries and that they closely guard these beans because of their special magic powers. He also gives you some of his tips on how to use the beans to make the most of their magic powers.

Excitedly you run home to get started doing your research.
