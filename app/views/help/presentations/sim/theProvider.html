Introduction to the The Provider Country: Middle-earth

In this story your may be asked to make decisions as the National Focal Point and publishing authority for the ABS-CH working for the Environment Ministry of the National Government of the Middle-earth.

Middle-earth is widely known to be rich in magical genetic resources. Middle-earth’s population is generally made up of several independent elves local communities. Magic genetic resources are important economically as well as culturally and traditionally for the Middle-earth and the local people. Many magic genetic resources and their wild relatives are found in the wild areas and elves territories. The elves local communities are the main caretakers of Middle-earth’s magic genetic resources and the sole holders of the associated magic knowledge. These genetic resource and the ecosystems where the grow and flourish also provide many wide ranging national and global socio-economic and environmental benefits,  from contributing to the fight against climate change to supporting the basic magic needs of the most marginalized and vulnerable of Middle-earth’s people like the Hobbits.

The provider country's goal is to protect the rights and well being of the inhabitants of your country and the environment while promoting opportunities for sustainable development of the Middle-earth. The provider country is are interested in getting the maximum monetary and non-monetary benefits from your magic genetic resources.
