Witchcraft Review the premier journal for serious wizards

YOU are the editor at Witchcraft Review

One day as you are editing your latest review when a USER walks into your office and shows you a very interesting research paper on a notoriously rare and magic bean. You would love to publish the paper in your magazine.

Witchcraft Review is not a Checkpoint. You agree to publish the paper in your journal. The article will come out in the following weeks magazine.

As an appointed checkpoint for the Nagoya Protocol, you ask the USER to provide information on prior informed consent, the source of the genetic resource, the establishment of mutually agreed terms, and/or the utilization of genetic resources. You secretly hope that he will have the IRCC, as it would make your job of filling the form for the checkpoint communique easier.

YOU MUST CHOOSE:

Make the potion without the beans
See if you can buy some at a store in Diagon Alley.
Make an expedition to Middle-earth to find and collect the beans yourself
