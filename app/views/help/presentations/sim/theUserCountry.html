The User Country: Designating Checkpoints

The CBD national focal point has just designated YOU as the National Focal Point and publishing authority for the Wizards’ kingdom, a country that ratified the Nagoya Protocol.

The User Country's goal is to comply with the Nagoya Protocol. Therefore you need to take measures to monitor the utilization of genetic resources, including the designation of one or more effective checkpoint. You also need to take measures to provide that magic genetic resources and associated magic knowledge utilized within Wizards’ Kingdom have been accessed with prior informed consent and mutually agreed terms as required by the ABS measures of the country providing such resources or knowledge.

You have been asked to advise on the designation of checkpoints for your country. YOU MUST CHOOSE:

Designate the national patent office responsible for magic genetic resources
Designate the Wizard World Review, a premier magic journal
Designate a research funding authority, Dumbledore Grants, for magic research grants. 
Designate the authority for market approval, Hedwig Market Approval Authority, responsible of giving the final approval before any potion can be commercialized. 
Decide to not designate any Checkpoints.
