Negotiating PIC and MAT

YOU are Gandalf, the CNA from Middle-earth, the provider country.

You are now ready to negotiate PIC and MAT. The user explains that he is using the beans for perfecting a potion and insists that the genetic resource he is wishing to access be kept strictly confidential.

YOU MUST CHOOSE:

Grant PIC and MAT only for research purposes. The contract also stipulates that no patenting of the genetic resources or associated magic knowledge is allowed.
Grant PIC and MAT for commercial purposes, patenting and commercializing the products is allowed in exchange for a share in all the profits. You agree to a milestone payment if a patent is granted.
You refuse to grant PIC or MAT. It is your genetic resource and you will not have it exploited.
